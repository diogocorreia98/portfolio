Tire Fleet Telemetry Pipeline

A complete telemetry pipeline case study built around fleet tire mileage data. The project starts with raw parquet ingestion, adds quality validation for duplicates, monotonicity breaks, impossible interval jumps, and mapping coverage, then finishes with fleet insights and a production-minded dimensional model.

Raw Volume 16.8M+ events
September Scope 3.1M records
Quality Violations 443,952 detected
Validated Output 49,200 tire-day rows
  • Recovered 1,133 mapped vehicles and identified 199 unmapped tires as a first-class monitoring gap.
  • Flagged 243 vehicles with 7+ consecutive inactive days to surface operational risk and low-utilization patterns.
  • Designed a star schema with fact and dimension tables, SCD Type 2 mounting history, auditability, and reprocessing support.
  • Documented production recommendations around quarantine tables, coverage checks, and daily data quality publishing.
  • Latest repository commit inspected: April 13, 2026.
